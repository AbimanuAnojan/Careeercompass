Streams for 1-10th Grade

Based on your current level, here are the academic streams you can pursue after 10th grade

🎓 Science Stream

Focuses on Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology. Ideal for careers in engineering, medicine, research, and technology.

Leads to fields like engineer, scientist, and (with Biology/PCB) doctor, and more.

💼 Commerce Stream

Covers Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, and Mathematics. Perfect for business, finance, and entrepreneurship.

Opens doors to CA, MBA, banking, and corporate careers.

🎨 Arts/Humanities Stream

Includes History, Geography, Political Science, Sociology, and languages. Great for creative and social sciences careers.

Leads to journalism, law, teaching, and social work professions.

Pathways for 11th Grade

Entrance exams and colleges you can target after 12th grade

🏛️ Engineering Entrance Exams

JEE Main & Advanced for IITs, NITs, and other engineering colleges. Focus on Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics.

Colleges: IIT Delhi, IIT Bombay, NIT Trichy, etc.

🏥 Medical Entrance Exams

NEET for MBBS, BDS. Requires Biology, Physics, Chemistry. Also AIIMS, JIPMER for specialized medical courses.

Colleges: AIIMS Delhi, CMC Vellore, AFMC Pune, etc.

📊 Other Competitive Exams

CLAT for Law, NATA for Architecture, UPSC for Civil Services, CA Foundation for Chartered Accountancy.

Various prestigious institutions across India.

Career Options for Undergraduates

Job opportunities and further studies after completing your bachelor's degree

💻 Software Engineer/Developer

Work in tech companies, develop applications, websites, and software solutions.

Companies: Google, Microsoft, Amazon, startups. Average salary: ₹6-15 LPA

🏥 Doctor/Medical Professional

Practice medicine, specialize in various fields like surgery, pediatrics, cardiology.

Hospitals, clinics, research. Pursue MD/MS for specialization.

📈 Business Analyst/Manager

Analyze business data, manage operations, strategic planning in corporate sector.

MNCs, consulting firms. Consider MBA for advancement.

Career Options for Postgraduates

Advanced roles and pathways after completing your postgraduate degree

Leadership and Management Roles

Move into decision-making roles across operations, strategy, consulting, and domain leadership.

Typical tracks: Product leadership, management consulting, corporate strategy.

Research and Academic Careers

Build a research profile, publish work, and pursue PhD, teaching, or specialist knowledge careers.

Typical tracks: Research scientist, assistant professor, policy researcher.

High-Skill Industry Specialization

Use your specialization to target advanced technical, analytical, and expert-level roles.

Typical tracks: AI specialist, finance expert, domain consultant, senior analyst.
